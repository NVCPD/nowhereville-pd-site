About the Department

The Nowhereville County Police Department (NVCPD) is the official law enforcement agency of No-where Ville, USA. We are dedicated to providing outstanding public safety and community engagement through fairness, discipline, and strength.

Divisions

  • High Command: Senior leadership and command staff.
  • BDU / CIRT: Tactical operations and high-risk incident response.
  • Plot Safety & Community Service Unit (PS&CSU): Maintains order during events and handles raiders and disturbances with plots.
  • Patrol Unit: Daily patrols, traffic enforcement, and emergency response.
  • Cybersecurity Team: Manages internal data and online security.
  • Canine Unit (K9): Trained K9 handlers for searches and raids.
  • Recruitment Unit: Oversees recruitment, onboarding, and officer development.
