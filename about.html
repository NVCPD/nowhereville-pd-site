Our Mission

The Nowhereville Police Department is committed to maintaining safety, order, and justice throughout Nowhereville. We work closely with the community to ensure transparency, fairness, and effective response to any critical incidents.

Every officer and unit within NVPD undergoes thorough training to uphold the highest standards of policing. Our divisions work tirelessly to protect the residents of Nowhereville and foster trust between law enforcement and the people.

Our Divisions

High Command

Senior leadership and command staff overseeing department operations and strategy.

Critical Incident Response Team (CIRT)

Handles tactical operations and responds to high-risk incidents across the town

Plot Safety & Community Service Unit (PS&CSU)

Maintains order during public events and responds to raiders or disturbances involving plots.

Patrol Unit

Conducts routine patrols, traffic enforcement, and first-response to emergencies.

Cybersecurity Team

Protects internal systems, monitors online threats, and maintains data security.

Canine Unit (K9)

Utilizes trained K9 handlers and dogs for searches, raids, and suspect apprehension.

Recruitment Unit

Manages recruitment, onboarding, and development of new and current officers.
