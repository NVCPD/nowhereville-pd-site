Our Mission

The Nowhereville County Police Department is committed to ensuring safety, order, and trust within the community. We respond to emergencies, enforce laws, and serve the people of Nowhereville with honor and professionalism.

Divisions of the NVCPD

High Command

Senior leadership and command staff overseeing department strategy and performance.

Critical Incident Response Team (CIRT)

Handles tactical operations and responds to high-risk or dangerous incidents.

Plot Safety & Community Service Unit (PS&CSU)

Maintains order during events and addresses raiders or disturbances around player plots.

Patrol Unit

Conducts routine patrols, enforces traffic laws, and responds to emergency calls daily.

Cybersecurity Team

Oversees internal systems, data protection, and digital infrastructure security.

Canine Unit (K9)

Trained K9 handlers for drug searches, raids, and suspect tracking during operations.

Recruitment Unit

Manages recruitment, onboarding, and training of new officers.
