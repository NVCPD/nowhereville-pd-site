Our Mission

The Nowhereville Police Department is committed to maintaining safety, justice, and peace for the citizens of Nowhereville County. Our goal is to respond quickly, act professionally, and build strong community trust.

Divisions

High Command

Senior leadership and command staff overseeing the department's direction and performance.

Critical Incident Response Team (CIRT)

Handles tactical operations and responds to high-risk or dangerous incidents.

Plot Safety & Community Service Unit (PS&CSU)

Manages public events, plot safety, and raider disturbances.

Patrol Unit

Conducts daily patrols, traffic enforcement, and responds to general emergencies.

Cybersecurity Team

Protects department systems, online infrastructure, and sensitive data.

Canine Unit (K9)

Trained handlers and K9s for suspect tracking, drug searches, and tactical raids.

Recruitment Unit

Oversees recruitment, officer onboarding, and training development.
