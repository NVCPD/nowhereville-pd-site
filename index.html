Welcome to the NVCPD Official Website

The Nowhereville County Police Department (NVCPD) is committed to serving our Town with professionalism, pride, and dedication.

Meet the Chief

Chief Shane Knight leads the NVCPD with over a decade of experience in law enforcement and public safety. His commitment to justice and leadership drives our mission forward.

Division Highlights

  • Patrol Unit: Responds to emergency calls and patrols the streets to keep Nowhereville safe.
  • Cybersecurity Team: Protects against digital threats and conducts cybercrime investigations.
  • K9 Unit: Works with trained dogs for search, detection, and criminal apprehension.
  • Critical Incident Response Team (CIRT): Handles high-risk situations and tactical operations.
  • Plot Safety & Community Service Unit: Ensures safe public spaces and community outreach.

  • Obey all local traffic laws and signals.
  • Stay aware of your surroundings at night.
