Welcome to the Nowhereville County Police Department

We are a fictional police department serving the virtual citizens of Nowhereville, USA within Roblox. Our department focuses on realism, safety, and roleplay standards.

Divisions

  • Cybersecurity Team: Investigates digital crimes and ensures network safety.
  • Critical Incident Response Team (CIRT): Handles high-risk threats and tactical deployments.
  • Patrol Unit: Frontline officers who maintain peace and respond to calls.
  • Recruitment Unit: Oversees hiring, onboarding, and training new probationary officers.
  • Plot Safety & Community Service Unit (PS&CSU): Maintains safety around player plots and prevents raiding.
  • Canine Unit (K9): Supports officers in locating illegal contraband and assisting in searches.
