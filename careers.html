Careers at NVCPD

The NVCPD is always looking for individuals who are committed to justice, teamwork, and integrity. If you’re interested in making a difference in Nowhereville, consider applying today.

Requirements

  • Must be at least 13 years old
  • Active and respectful within the community
  • Roblox account in good standing
  • Willing to follow orders and chain of command
  • Discord required for communication
  • MUST BE ACTIVE.

Open Positions

  • Probationary Officer – Entry-level patrol and support role
  • K9 Handler – Partnered with trained canines
  • Recruitment Officer – Help onboard new members and run interviews
  • Cybersecurity Officer – Monitor and report community threats
  • PS&CSU Officer – Community outreach and safety enforcement
  • CIRT Operative – Critical incident response, high-risk calls
